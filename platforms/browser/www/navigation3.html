Connecting to Device

Task 3: Rotor Gestures
Part 1: Rotor Gestures
Rotor Gestures allow you to change the way you navigate pages or lists. To change rotor gestures, imagine the rotor gesture as a dial that you turn with your thumb and index finger. Rotate both index and thumb without lifting your fingers. A "ssss" sound should play, followed by the name of the item.
Part 2: Using Rotor Gestures
The current rotor gesture can be activated by flicking your finger up or down.
Part 3: Practice
Rotate both index and thumb to change Rotor Gestures. Activate the rotor gesture by flicking your finger up or down.
