Task 2: Creating a Message
Part 1: Composing a Message
To compose a message , select the "New Message" button by tapping on the top right of the screen, or swiping left several times until you hear "New Message". Then, double tapping the screen will bring you to the compose message interface.
Part 2: Compose Message Interface (I)
This interface is where you will write the message. By default, the "To" field is selected, with the keyboard already displayed on the bottom half of the screen. Keying in the name or number of the contact will prompt autocomplete results to show up on the middle of the screen. If you wish, tap on the autocomplete results to confirm the contact.
Part 3: Compose Message Interface (II)
Once done with the "To" field, tap on the empty part of the screen. Then, swipe right until you hear "message". Double tap the screen, and then type your message. Once done, tap on the top right of the screen, or swipe right until your hear "Send button", then double tap the screen.
Part 4: Continuing a Conversation
To reply to a conversation, first enter the conversation page by selecting a conversation on the main page, then double tapping the screen. Then, swipe right until you hear "message". Once done, swipe right until you hear "Send button", and proceed to double tap the screen.
