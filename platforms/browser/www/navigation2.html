Connecting to Device

Task 2: Scrolling
Part 1: Three-finger scrolling
To scroll through a vertical list, find a list item, then flick three fingers up or down. To scroll through a horizontal list, find a list item, then flick two fingers left or right.
Part 2: Three-finger scrolling
To navigate through multiple pages (like on the home screen), flick three fingers left or right. To move to the next page, flick three fingers right. To move to the previous page, flick three fingers left. Give it a try here.
Part 3: Practice
Scroll by flicking three fingers up or down.
