Connecting to Device

Task 1: The Messages Application
Part 1: Finding the Messages Application
The Messages Application is used primarily to send messages. To access this application, tap on the Messages Application, which is a green square typically located at the top left corner of the second page of the home screen. Once you hear "Messages", double tap the screen.
Part 2: The Messages Interface
The main page of the Messages Application displays a list of all the messages received or sent, organized into conversations with your contacts. You can either tap, or swipe right/left to select a conversation - upon selecting a conversation, VoiceOver will read out the contact and the content of the last message.
Part 3: Messaging
Double tapping once you have selected a conversation on the main page will open a separate page detailing the chat history between you and the contact. Tap, or swipe right/left to messages in order to read them.
